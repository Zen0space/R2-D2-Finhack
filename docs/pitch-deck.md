## Slide 4 — The Solution · 60 seconds

**Visual**
- Three vertical pillars, equal width
- Each pillar: heritage-glyph icon at top, pillar name, 2-3 bullet supports
- Below pillars: a single horizontal architecture band showing TNG eWallet as foundation

**Headline** (Cormorant Garamond 40pt)
> Three pillars, one rail.

**Pillar 1 — RAILS**
- Icon: stylized rotation-arrow circle (tabung-gold)
- **TNG eWallet auto-deduct**
- Members already have the rails in their pocket
- No new app install, no KYC friction beyond what TNG provides
- Contribution hits the pool on a scheduled date without intervention

**Pillar 2 — LEDGER**
- Icon: open-book with horizontal rules (maroon)
- **Visible record, not hidden**
- Every member sees every transaction in real time
- Immutable ledger — contributions and payouts are both public to the group
- Trust does not depend on one person remembering correctly

**Pillar 3 — TRUST**
- Icon: songket-octagon with checkmark inset (gold)
- **Reputation you can carry**
- Each member has a visible trust score: paid-on-time vs. missed
- Trust carries across tabung — your reputation follows you
- AI Penasihat advises in BM-first, grounded in your actual tabung state

**Foundation band** (Inter small caps, cream on maroon)
> Built on: TNG eWallet · Postgres · Better Auth · AWS S3 · Claude API
