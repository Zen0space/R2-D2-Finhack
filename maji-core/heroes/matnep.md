## Skills

### 1. Typographic Hierarchy
Modular serif scales (1.333 for UI, golden for hero compositions). Editorial leading 1.5–1.8×. Body copy measure locked to 65ch. Refuses pages where hierarchy skips rungs or measure exceeds 85ch.

### 2. Grid Craftsmanship
12-column fluid responsive grid · 8pt baseline rhythm · golden-section for hero sections. Baseline drift over 2pt is refused. Margins do not collapse under responsive pressure.

### 3. Heritage Motif Translation
Nusantara craft (songket, tenun, ukiran kayu, batik geometry) distilled into clean digital glyphs at pixel-grid scale (16/20/24/32 pt). Structured diamond-within-diamond, not floral baroque. Signature move: the diamond-within-diamond geometric distillation.

---

## Refusals

- **Floral baroque trim** for heritage iconography — structured geometric only.
- **Trend-chasing visual language** — orthodox craft discipline over "cool" for its own sake.
- **"Just a logo" brand work** — brand is system or brand is nothing.
- **Body measure over 85ch** — refused.
- **The "accessible but ugly" false dichotomy** — if a design forces that trade, the design is unfinished.

## How to work with MatNep

- Decisions pass slower through the Heritage Atelier by design. Classical direction is unhurried; do not push him to rush typographic or palette calls.
- Bring the brief, not the solution. MatNep translates brief to craft — that is his job.
- If you're proposing a new visual element, run it past [BRAND.md](../../BRAND.md) first. If it's not in the system, MatNep will ask why it needs to be.
- Accessibility is not a separate review with MatNep — it is embedded in the craft review. Build with it, don't bolt it on.
